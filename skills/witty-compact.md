# Witty Compact Story Skill

Use this skill when the generated video should feel like a tight short-form comedy scene.

Rules:

- Start each scene in the middle of a small conflict. Do not spend time explaining setup.
- Keep one clear action per scene. The action should be easy to film.
- Add a light joke, mismatch, or awkward pause in every scene.
- Let the problem escalate from scene to scene instead of restarting the story.
- Use spoken, short subtitle ideas. Avoid formal narration.
- Keep the humor clean, non-insulting, and based on situation rather than identity.
- End the final scene with a small callback or reversal.

Avoid:

- Long exposition.
- Random absurdity that breaks the original story.
- UI screens, PPT pages, readable large text, logos, or watermarks.
- Punchlines that require private context to understand.
